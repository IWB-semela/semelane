What We Offer

At IWB, we combine cutting-edge technology with sustainable practices to offer businesses and individuals a reliable way to recycle electronics, recover data, and ensure digital security. Our innovative solutions provide businesses, government organizations, and individual clients with secure and environmentally friendly e-waste management.

1. Electronic Recycling & E-Waste Management

We specialize in safe disposal and recycling of old electronic devices, including RAM, hard drives, and motherboard components. Our zero-landfill policy ensures that e-waste is either repurposed or responsibly processed to minimize environmental impact.

  • Certified E-Waste Disposal in compliance with international environmental regulations.
  • Component Recovery & Refurbishing to give electronics a second life.
  • Eco-friendly disposal techniques to reduce toxic waste impact.

2. Data Recovery Services

Lost important files? We provide advanced data recovery solutions for individuals and businesses. Whether it is a hardware failure, accidental deletion, or software corruption, we can restore your data safely.

  • Hard Drive & SSD Recovery: Recover lost files from damaged storage devices.
  • RAID & Server Data Recovery: Specialized solutions for enterprise data loss.
  • Deleted File & Partition Recovery: Restore accidentally deleted documents.

3. Secure Data Destruction & Digital Privacy

In an era of cyber threats, simply deleting files is not enough. Our data destruction solutions permanently erase sensitive information using military-grade algorithms, ensuring that no one can recover it.

  • Hard Drive & SSD Shredding: Complete destruction of storage devices.
  • Secure Digital Wiping: Data is overwritten multiple times to prevent recovery.
  • Compliance with GDPR & Data Protection Laws: Ideal for businesses handling confidential information.

4. IT Asset Disposal (ITAD)

We offer secure IT asset disposal solutions for businesses upgrading their technology. Our process ensures that old equipment is disposed of responsibly, refurbished, or recycled while maintaining strict data security protocols.

  • Certified Asset Disposal Reports: Ideal for corporate compliance.
  • Refurbishment & Donation Programs: Giving electronics a second life in underprivileged communities.
  • Corporate Sustainability Partnerships: Helping businesses meet green IT goals.

5. Cybersecurity Solutions

Our cybersecurity experts provide consulting services to businesses and individuals, ensuring that their data remains protected from cyber threats, malware, and unauthorized access.

  • Network Security Audits: Identify vulnerabilities in business networks.
  • End-to-End Encryption Solutions: Secure communication and data transfers.
  • Dark Web Monitoring: Detect if sensitive data has been leaked online.

  • Industry-Leading Security: Advanced technology to protect your data.
  • Trusted by Businesses & Governments: Proven track record in cybersecurity and e-waste management.
  • ISO & GDPR Compliant: Our processes meet global standards.
