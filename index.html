About IWB

IWB (Innovative Waste & Bytes) is a Lesotho-based electronic recycling company established in 2024. Our mission is to reduce e-waste by responsibly recycling computer components while ensuring data security through innovative software solutions.

Our Founders

The company was founded by Kenneth with an initial investment of M100,000. Later, he partnered with Shadrack, who joined as the second CEO. Together, they expanded IWB into one of the leading electronic recycling companies in Southern Africa.

Our Mission

To provide sustainable electronic recycling solutions and secure data management services, ensuring that technology is disposed of responsibly while safeguarding digital information.

Our Vision

To become the leading provider of electronic waste management and cybersecurity solutions in Africa by 2030.

Our Values

  • Sustainability: Reducing electronic waste through innovative recycling methods.
  • Security: Ensuring data is destroyed or recovered safely.
  • Innovation: Developing cutting-edge tools for data protection and hardware optimization.
  • Integrity: Providing ethical and environmentally friendly services.
